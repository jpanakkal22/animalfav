Favorite Animal

Dogs

Dogs are considered "Man's Best Friend" for a very good reason. I have personally owned a dog for over 11 years, and to say that she has been a "best friend" is an understatement.


Dogs are a member of the genus, Canus or what we consider Canines, and closely resemble wolves. What makes them wonderful pets are their behaviors, physical traits and senses that we as humans find useful to our lives.


Many owners like myself look to dogs for personal protection because of their barking, hearing, sense of smell and ability to be trained to obey commands. Not only these, but most dogs have a loving and pleasing nature that makes them a great fit amongst other animals as additions to family households.
